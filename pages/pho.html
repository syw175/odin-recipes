Ingredients

  • Marrow bones and Meaty bones
  • Thinly sliced raw meat
  • Yellow onions
  • Whole garlic
  • Fresh ginger
  • Whole spices: star anise, cloves, cinnamon sticks, black cardamom pods, fennel seeds, coriander seeds
  • Salt
  • Fish sauce
  • Sugar

Instructions

  1. Cook the mirepoix. To a medium stockpot over medium heat, add the olive oil and shallots. Cook the shallots for approximately 5 minutes, stirring often. Add the white parts of a few green onions, the minced garlic, and ginger. Stir well to combine and cook for 1-2 more minutes.
  2. Add pre-made broth or stock. Add your favorite store-bought broth or stock to the pot and bring to a simmer.
  3. Add some flavor. Add 1-2 whole star anise, soy sauce, and fish sauce to the broth. Cover. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Remove the star anise. Try not to forget this part.
  5. Season to taste. When it comes to Pho, you do you. Add more salt, more sugar, more fish sauce, etc.
